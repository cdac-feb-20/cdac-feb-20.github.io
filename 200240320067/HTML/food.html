1:Kibble/Dry

Dry food is the most economical type of commercial dog food, and this is the reason that many owners choose it for their dog.

It also lasts for a long time and does not need to be refrigerated. Dry food can also help to keep your dogâ€™s teeth healthy, since chewing crunchy dry food

2:Semi-Mois

Commercial dog foods shaped like pork chops, burgers, or other meaty foods are called semi-moist foods.

These kinds of foods are the least nutritional of all dog foods and contain many artificial flavors and coloringsThey can be given to your dog as an occasional treat,

but they should not be considered a diet in themselves, as they do not provide the nutrition that your pup requires.






3.Raw

A raw diet consists of raw meat, preferably with some bones (never cooked bones, only raw) and organs mixed in, as bones are a natural source of phosphorus and calcium.
